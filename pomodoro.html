How to Use the Pomodoro Timer?

The Pomodoro technique helps boost productivity by working in intervals of 25 minutes followed by short breaks.

Steps:

1. Click "Start Pomodoro Cycle" to begin a 25-minute focused work session.

2. After 25 minutes, take a short 5-minute break. The timer will automatically switch to this break.

3. Every 4 Pomodoros (work sessions), take a longer 15-minute break.

4. You can reset the cycle anytime by clicking the "Reset" button.
